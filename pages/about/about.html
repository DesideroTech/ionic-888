Instructions:

  1. Go to the Calculate tab.
  2. Input your height in centimeters.
  3. Input your weight in kilograms in the row below.
  4. Input your age if you are over 18 in the row below. (The calculator will not work if you are under 18).
  5. Select your sex in the row below.
  6. Then, select your physical activity level.
  7. Then, click on calculate
  8. Your results will appear on the DCI indicator.
