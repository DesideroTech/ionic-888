About The Company

"We create what everyone wants. We guarantee to deliver our clients the technology they want!"

Desidero's name came from the combination of the creators last names. Upon searching desidero is an italian word which means I want, I wish or I desire. The goal of Desiderio Tech is to create apps and technology that the people want, wish, and desire. Furthermore, to change the perspective of people towards technology and create a significant contribution to the tech industry. The company aims to produce practical apps that users will need during this pandemic and eventually branch out.

Desidero's first project is the daily calculator intake app which aims to help guide users to their ideal daily calorie intake to maintain a healthy lifestyle amidst this pandemic. The pandemic has limited the suse of public facilities such as gyms, courts, etc. in order to stay fit during this pandemic, we have created an app to help you manage your eating lifestyle to ensure a healthy body.

Daily Calorie Intake Calculator App

The calorie calculator can help you in determining how many calories should you eat a day - or what your starting point is if you want to gain or lose weight.
